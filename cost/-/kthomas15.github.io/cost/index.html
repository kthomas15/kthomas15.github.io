The Cost to Attend UNL


About

Looking to attend a university out of state? This data consists of the average cost to attend UNL for a student who is not a resident in the state of Nebraska. The cost provided does not includes additional fees. Take a look at how the tution rate has changed throughout the years for residents who do not live in Nebraska.
